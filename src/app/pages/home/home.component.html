Welcome to Blockwork. BlockWork is a distributed freelancing platform that enables freelancers to connect with clients directly. The importance of this system is, it removes the use of a third-party involvement. So, freelancers or their clients don’t need to pay an extra fee to a third-party organization. This is possible because the proposed system runs on a blockchain.
