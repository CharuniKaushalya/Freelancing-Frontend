Freelancer fail all redo requests

If a freelancer fail all the redo requests after QA testing and project was QA tested by the client, freelancer will get paid

percent

If the project has a separate QA,

QA can decide the completed percentage of the current milestone. That percentage from the current milestone payment will be paid to the freelancer.

Both QA and Freelancer contracts will be automatically cancelled.


Freelancer fail to meet a deadline

If a freelancer fail to meet a milestone deadline, freelancers's milestone pay will be reduced by

percent for each extended day.

If a freelancer fail to meet a milestone deadline for a project worth over $

and don't submit work done after

days, contract will be automatically cancelled.

If a freelancer fail to meet a milestone deadline for a project worth less than $

and don't submit work done after

days, contract will be automatically cancelled.



Freelancer won't get paid for the current milestone.

If freelancer contract has a linked QA contract, that QA contract will also be cancelled. QA won't be paid for the current milestone either.


QA fail to meet a deadline

If a QA fail to meet a milestone deadline, freelancers's milestone pay will be reduced by

percent for each extended day.

If a QA fail to meet a milestone deadline for a project worth over $

and don't submit work done after

days, contract will be automatically cancelled.

If a QA fail to meet a milestone deadline for a project worth less than $

and don't submit work done after

days, contract will be automatically cancelled.



QA won't get paid for the current milestone.

After cancellation of the QA contract, clients can decide to hire a new QA or QA the project themselves.
